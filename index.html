Mario Kart 7 is a racing game for the Nintendo 3DS. It is the ninth installment of the Mario Kart series overall, the seventh in the main series (hence the game's title), and the third on a handheld console. In the game, players are able to exchange Ghost Data and play online in multiplayer mode.
